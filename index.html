Story
about me

Inesa Balic
Klicic Art

             My name is Inesa Balic-Klicic, I was born and raised in Bihac, Bosnia and Herzegovina. Like so many artists I was a "special snowflake" during my childhood and most of my time I have spend reading/writing poetry and novels, but most of all I was always drawing and painting something. When I look back, my artistic side was my path and my stumbling stone through out my childhood. I have always been wondering and asking myself is it possible to live in Bosnia and to make a living doing what you love. I had to overcome many obstacles and I was always fighting windmills, but now I see it was the best possible journey because all the hardship made everything more valuable and worthwhile.
              Hometown Bihac is my biggest inspiration, and I honestly believe that all the artists who come to Bihac fall in love with this town and its river Una. One cannot resist to be in love with it, it truly captivates you.
